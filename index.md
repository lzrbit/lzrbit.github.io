## About Me
Hello! My name is Zirui Li (李子睿).  I am a PhD candidate working under the supervision of Prof. Jianwei Gong in School of Mechanical Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology. I am also a Visiting Researcher at TU Delft under the supervision of Prof. Bart van Arem, Prof. Victor Knoop and Prof. Meng Wang.
{: style="text-align: justify;"}

My research interests cover the combination of decision making and motion planning algorithms. My focus is more about interactive driver behaviour modelling and learning-based motion planning algorithms with [CARLA simulation platform](http://carla.org/).
{: style="text-align: justify;"}

## Research Interests
* Interaction-aware Decision Making
* The combination of decision making and motion planning algorithms
* Graph Neural Network (GNN)
* Transferable Driver Behaviour Modelling
* Machine Learning Techniques that Improve the Performance and Efficiency of Classical Motion Planning Methods
